DESIGN PORTFOLIO

Welcome to the Robin Baron Design Portfolio!

Robin is proud of the work she has done over the last 30 years. We invite you to come in and visit some of the unique and beautiful properties that Robin and her team have had the privilege of designing for our wonderful clients. Many of the spaces feature Robin’s custom furniture, lighting and rugs, which were designed specifically for each client.

As you visually walk through our client’s amazing homes, we hope you will be delighted and inspired.

We would love to help make your dream home a reality! Our design team is ready to collaborate with you through our Design By The Hour, Design By The Room, or our Full Service Design services. Visit our Design Services page and find the perfect plan to fit your needs.

We look forward to connecting with you!
