Astor LED Chandelier

$570.00

Share

Please contact customer service for information about stock or to place an order.

Robin Baron's design for George Kovacs, a Minka Group Company, uses a gorgeous soft gold finish on its beautiful swirling form and draws inspiration from Robin's iconic Looped Hardware. The Astor LED Chandelier is stylish and subtle, and its LEDs are sure to provide beautiful light to your space while still dressing up your room. With two available diameters, you're sure to find the right size for your space, completing your room with a jewelry-esque chandelier hanging from your ceiling.

  • Part of the Astor Collection
  • Overall dimensions for small: 32"Dia. X 21"H
  • Drop range for small: 29.5" to 65.5"
  • Overall dimensions for large: 38"Dia. x 21"H
  • Drop range for large: 29.5" to 65.5"
  • Made with steel, aluminum, and plastic
  • Available in a soft gold finish
  • Weight: 7.01 lbs. for small; 7.56 lbs. for large

  • ETL Certified; rated for damp locations
  • Dimmable
  • Adjustable for sloped ceilings
  • Typically ships within one week
